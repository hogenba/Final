The Impossible Maze

Go through random mazes, find the objective, and get there in time!!!

By: Blake Hogen, AJ Diehl, and Aiden Miner

World

Each world within the game is generated randomly, with random wall placement, objective placement, distraction #'s, and time to complete the level.

Time Limit

Make sure to get to the objective before time runs out, otherwise, it's game over!

Hero

This is your hero, you must guide him through the maze in order to win...

Objective

There's only one objective. You have little time to get there! Good luck, it's called the Impossible Maze for a reason!

Maze Block

This is a maze block. It is meant to hide the objective. Have a careful eye to find it!

Distraction!

This is a distraction, they spawn randomly, and move quickly. They block out the Hero, and the objective. You have to ignore them to get to the end in time.

Game Over!

Makes sure you get to the objective in time, otherwise it's game over!
